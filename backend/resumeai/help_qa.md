# JobCrafts AI - Help & FAQ

## What is JobCrafts AI?
JobCrafts AI is an AI-powered career platform that helps job seekers optimize their resumes, generate cover letters, prepare for interviews, and improve their LinkedIn profiles. It uses advanced AI to tailor your application materials for any job posting.

## Features

### Resume Upload & Parsing
- Upload PDF or DOCX resumes (max 10MB)
- Automatic text extraction for use in other features
- Resumes are stored in your account

### ATS Optimization
- Optimize your resume for Applicant Tracking Systems
- Enter a job description and get an AI-rewritten resume
- Receive an ATS score, keyword breakdown, missing keywords, and improvement suggestions
- Download optimized resume as DOCX

### Cover Letter Generator
- Create personalized cover letters in seconds
- Enter company name, job title, job description, and preferred tone
- AI generates professional cover letters (220-380 words)

### Interview Prep
- Get AI-generated interview questions tailored to your resume and target role
- Suggested answers with STAR framework for behavioral questions
- Add your own custom questions for AI to answer
- Tips and key points for each question

### LinkedIn Optimizer
- Optimize your LinkedIn headline, about section, and experience bullets
- Enter your target role and current profile text
- Get multiple headline options, about versions, experience rewrites, and recommended skills
- Optimizations are saved in your Versions page

### Career Gap Analysis
- Explain career gaps professionally
- Get resume entry suggestions, LinkedIn entry, interview answers
- Skill gap assessment, certifications, learning roadmap, and action plan

### Job Analysis
- Paste a job description to extract keywords
- See which keywords are in your resume vs missing
- Get coverage percentage

### Portfolio (Pro Plan)
- Create a shareable portfolio page
- Add experience, projects, skills
- Share link with recruiters
- Download resume from portfolio
